Our Mission

Conventional McMaster parasite testing has limitations ranging from cost, inefficiency, and even inaccessibility to farmers residing in rural areas. Our automated egg-counting solution leverages AI to deliver a rapid parasite count without the need for an internet connection or access to a veterinarian facility. With this technology, our mission is to incentivize frequent testing and prevent the need for more extreme measure like deworming or herd infections.
